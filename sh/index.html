About

Rules

  1. You have 45 minutes to locate as many locations on campus in the scavenger hunt. You and your team must take a #selfie at the location and submit photo to the Kudos wall in Salesforce. (hint - Admission staff in both UG and GR, download the Salesforce app on your phone.).

So do you squeeze in one more location or do you play it safe to make sure you are in the last location before time expires?!? WHAT. DO. YOU. DO?

  3. The team with the most locations captured (and in Kudos chatter via Salesforce app) will be deemed the winner with a prize!
